Intract through the whole meal

When all players are present, everyone receives a card. The detective tries to figure out the action of each person, everyone else tries to figure out who the detective is. Throughout the supper, throughout the conversation each person shares their day as if lived by the character card. The person must share their action four times throughout the meal. If the detective figures out one of the actions of anyone present, that detective wins. If at any time someone points out who the detective is, that person wins.
